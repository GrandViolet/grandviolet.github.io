Who is Grand Violet?

Grand Violet is a composer. She writes music (mainly jazz) which are available on Spotify, Apple Music, or anywhere else you listen to music. Grand Violet is a musician. She performs songs, both her own compositions and others, on the piano and sometimes singing. Grand Violet is a writer. She not only writes music, but also writes short stories, poems, and other works. She also enjoys writing code in C++, Python, and C. Grand Violet is a designer. She creates mechanical devices which she then 3D prints. She also designs graphics for her album art and promotional posts. Grand Violet is an artist. Being an artist bleeds into every aspect of her life.
