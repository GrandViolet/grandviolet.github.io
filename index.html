Who is Grand Violet?

Grand Violet is a composer. She writes music (mainly jazz) which is available on Spotify, Apple Music, or anywhere else you listen to music. Grand Violet is a musician. She performs songs, both her own compositions and others, on the piano and sometimes singing. Grand Violet is a writer. She not only writes music, but also writes short stories, poems, and other works. She also enjoys writing code in C++, Python, and C. Grand Violet is a designer. She creates mechanical devices which she then 3D prints. She also designs graphics for her album art and promotional posts. Grand Violet is an artist. Being an artist bleeds into every aspect of her life.

Music

Her new songs release on YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, or anywhere else you listen to music. Her most recent release was Selections from Grand Violet, which released on March 20th, 2026. The album is a compilation of her greatest hits since 2020. She is currently writing new music which will be released soon!

Crafts

She is also a crafter. She goes to craft shows around the New Jersey area to sell her original 3D printed designs. Keep an eye out and you might see her selling under the name Grand Violet Crafts. So far, she has sold small trinket boxes and fidget toys with plans to do more in the future.

Store

Grand Violet also has merchandise for sale. She is currently selling CDs and vinyl records of the Selections from Grand Violet album. CDs are $10 plus shipping and records are $50 plus shipping. Please send an email to grandvioletoffical@gmail.com if you're interested!
